What to do when your EV charger installer goes out of business

This is the “orphaned charger” problem. Here’s how to regain control of your site and get chargers back online.

Step 1: Gather the essentials

  • Charger make/model + serial numbers (photos help)
  • Network provider (if any) and account email used for setup
  • Breaker panel location + who has access
  • Any service contracts or invoices from the original install

Step 2: Identify whether it’s power, network, or hardware

Many “dead” stations are actually network-disconnected or stuck in a software loop. A quick diagnostic can isolate the root cause and prevent wasted replacements.

Step 3: Decide your path

Take over maintenance

Find a service partner to own diagnostics, repairs, and ongoing inspections.

Re-network

If the network provider is the blocker, migrate or re-provision devices.

Replace

Only after diagnosis confirms replacement is cheaper than repair.

Preventative maintenance checklist for Melbourne HOA managers

Most charging downtime is preventable. Here’s a simple checklist to keep stations safe and reliable.

Monthly checks (15–30 minutes)

  • Inspect cables for fraying, kinks, or exposed conductors
  • Check holsters, strain reliefs, and mounting hardware for cracks/looseness
  • Wipe down screens and vents (dust buildup causes overheating)
  • Confirm at least one successful charge session per unit

Quarterly checks (site-level)

  • Review error logs/alerts from your network provider (if applicable)
  • Verify connectivity (Wi‑Fi/cellular/backhaul) and signal strength
  • Check breaker panel labeling and access process for after-hours incidents
  • Confirm signage, striping, and ADA/parking compliance where required

Safety reminder: If you see exposed wiring, overheating, or repeated breaker trips, take the unit out of service and schedule professional repair.

Why is my ChargePoint station offline? (And how to reset it)

If your station is dark, frozen, or rejecting cards, start here. This guide is written for property managers who need uptime fast.

1) Confirm what “offline” means

  • Dark screen (no power or internal fault)
  • Frozen UI (software lockup)
  • Network offline (cell/Wi‑Fi/backhaul issue)
  • Payment errors (reader, backend, or configuration issue)

2) Safe reset checklist (property manager friendly)

  1. Take photos of the screen/error and the unit label/serial (helps support & techs).
  2. If you have access to the breaker panel, perform a controlled power cycle (off 60 seconds → on).
  3. Wait 5–10 minutes for boot and connectivity to re-establish.
  4. Test with a known-good vehicle/connector if available.

Safety note: If you see frayed cables, exposed wiring, or cracked holsters, do not operate the station. Mark it out of service and escalate to repair.

3) When it’s time to call a pro

  • Repeated offline events after resets
  • Physical damage, overheating, tripping breakers
  • Multiple units down at the same site
  • “Orphaned” installs (original installer unresponsive/out of business)
